# IFRS 9 Financial Instruments

Source: [IFRS Foundation official page](https://www.ifrs.org/issued-standards/list-of-standards/ifrs-9-financial-instruments/)

## What it is

The IFRS standard for financial instruments. It covers classification and measurement, impairment, and hedge accounting.

## Impairment relevance

- IFRS 9 added impairment requirements based on expected credit losses for financial assets and commitments to extend credit.
- The impairment chapter is part of the completed IFRS 9 package issued in July 2014.
- IFRS 9 is the standard you use when the question is about accounting treatment of credit losses under IFRS.

## Historical note

- IFRS 9 was designed to replace IAS 39 in phases.
- The IFRS Foundation page records the standard history, including the addition of impairment requirements and later amendments.
