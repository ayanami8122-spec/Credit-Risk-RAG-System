# Finance Risk Glossary

This glossary normalizes the core terms used across the RAG and Agent apps.

## Core terms

- **PD**: Probability of Default.
- **LGD**: Loss Given Default.
- **EAD**: Exposure at Default.
- **EL / ECL**: Expected Loss / Expected Credit Loss.
- **CAR**: Capital Adequacy Ratio.
- **RWA**: Risk-Weighted Assets.
- **IRB**: Internal Ratings-Based approach.
- **Output Floor**: A minimum floor applied to model-based RWAs relative to the standardised approach.
- **SICR**: Significant Increase in Credit Risk.
- **CCF**: Credit Conversion Factor.
- **RAROC**: Risk-Adjusted Return on Capital.
- **NPL**: Non-Performing Loan.
- **Leverage Ratio**: Tier 1 capital divided by total exposure.

## Use rule

Keep explanations short, plain, and finance-facing. Do not turn this into a textbook chapter.
