# China Financial Supervision Overview

Official source anchors:

- [National Financial Regulatory Administration](https://www.nfra.gov.cn/)
- [People's Bank of China](https://www.pbc.gov.cn/)

## Purpose

This short note gives the RAG system a minimal domestic supervision layer so the model can answer China-facing finance questions with the right institutional framing.

## What this note covers

- The NFRA is the main prudential supervisor for banking and insurance in China.
- Its remit includes supervision of capital adequacy, risk management, internal control, and information disclosure for regulated institutions.
- The PBC contributes the macro-prudential and monetary-policy side of the domestic framework.

## How to use it in RAG

Use this note only for high-level domestic framing. For detailed legal or regulatory answers, prefer adding the exact official document first.
